# Nike Sales Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes Nike sales performance across different regions and product categories to understand revenue trends and business performance.

---

## Objective
To explore sales data and identify patterns in regional performance, product demand, and overall revenue distribution.

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Data cleaning and organization  
- Pivot tables and basic analysis  

---

## Key Areas of Analysis
- Regional sales performance  
- Product category performance  
- Revenue distribution patterns  
- Identification of high and low-performing segments  

---

## Key Insights
- Sales performance varies significantly across regions  
- Certain product categories consistently generate higher revenue  
- Clear patterns exist in sales distribution across markets  

---

## Outcome
-This analysis provides insights into sales behaviour and highlights areas of strong perfomance and potential improvement across regions and product categories
